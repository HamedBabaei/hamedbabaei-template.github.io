About Me

I am a first-year M.Sc. student in computer science at the University of Tabriz in the field of Soft Computing and Artificial Intelligence. My research interests include Artificial Intelligence, Machine learning, Natural Language Processing, and Deep Learning.

Currently, I am working on four research projects which three of them are related to each other.

  • In the first project, I try to make a recommender system to recommend products to users in Persian websites.
  • The second project is about Question Answering System for customer services in Persian websites.
  • The third project is a customized Search Engine for websites.
  • The fourth project is about online Machine Translation from Persian to English and English to Persian for translating academic papers.
None of these projects funded or is for a specific company I run these projects based on my enthusiastic about AI. All of these projects are big for me, but as a friend of mine said Try More not Less.
